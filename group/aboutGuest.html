About
Introduction 
Unit Sukan acts as a mover and coordinator and implements sports activities for
students and staff of UiTM Perlis. Through planned sports programs and activities, the target
Unit Sukan is to involve as many as 80% of the campus community active in sports activities.
Unit Sukan also plays a role in enhancing the image of UiTM Perlis through outstanding
achievements in the field of sports. To strengthen and raise the name of UiTM Perlis in the field
of sports, the Sports Unit of the Student Affairs Division of UiTM Perlis has made plans for
sports activities and plans for the improvement and upgrading of sports facilities that will be
implemented in 2015. Unit Sukan logo is a round concept based on UiTM's efforts
to raise the standard of sports at the world level. UiTM logo in the middle symbolizes that the
excellence of the university as the main generator of sports activities and rice flowers
symbolizes the bond of sports as a high sporting aspect with noble values.


Mission 
To make UiTM Perlis a University with a strong sports culture and as an effective catalyst 
for change in building a sports, healthy and progressive society.


Vision 
Provide quality infrastructure in assisting the development and development of sports by 
adopting an international science, technology and sports facilities approach.
